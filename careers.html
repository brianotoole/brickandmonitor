Careers

WordPress Developer

OVERVIEW

We’re looking for a WordPress Developer who will work closely alongside our in-house team of designers, strategists and producers to create large-scale, award-winning websites. If developing Awwwards-worthy code for a variety of cool companies makes you excited, we’re the perfect spot for you!

RESPONSIBILITIES
  • Work closely with an in-house team of designers, project managers, and digital strategists to take websites and applications from concept to working product for a variety of clients across multiple industries.
  • Help maintain and add functionality to existing websites.
  • Produce effective business-oriented solutions based on organizational goals.
  • Communicate ideas clearly and in a manner that can be understood by clients and ‘non-techie’ personnel.
  • Install, customize and maintain plug-ins and 3rd-party applications.
  • Help refine and define 829 Studios’ processes and workflows.
MUST-HAVES
  • Expert knowledge of the WordPress framework.
  • Four or more years of experience developing front end web sites and applications using HTML5, CSS3, PHP, JavaScript and JQuery.
  • Familiarity with UNIX infrastructure.
  • Strong grasp of media queries and responsive design.
  • Familiarity with a version control system (we use Git) and deployment procedures.
  • Working knowledge of the Adobe suite of products for extracting graphical assets.
  • Strong problem-solving skills.
NICE-TO-HAVES
  • Familiarity with Amazon Web Services
  • Knowledge of Google Analytics
  • Please, no staffing agencies!
