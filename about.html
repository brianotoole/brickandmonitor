Our Custom Development Process

Research

Understanding competitive landscapes, markets, industries, behaviors and needs to inform an effective project strategy.

Discovery

Discussing project timeline and key milestones. Learning and understanding the problems to be solved.

Planning & Content

Refining the functional requirements for the Design and Development stages and planning the content architecture.

Design & Workflow

Applying the learnings from planning session into a visual represenation of end product. Then, creating the development roadmap.

Development

Implementing the functionality decided upon in the Discovery, Research, Planning and Design stages. Internal quality testing and verification of feature completion.

Refine & Launch

Reviewing the functionality of the product with the customer against the established acceptance criteria in a testing environment and launching once approved.
